Our Mission

A stronger and more
hopeful future

Our mission is to create a stronger and more hopeful future by empowering individuals and communities through educational and sustainable poverty alleviation initiatives. Since 2008, Eclat Beginnings has assisted in alleviating poverty through a variety of services offered in partnership with local organisations in affected communities.

Secondary School Program

Financial support to underprivileged children for post-primary education, expanding opportunities and improving their future prospects.

Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skills training, and a variety of economic opportunities to build confidence, improve livelihoods, and strengthen families and communities.

Vocational Training

Apprenticeships and hands-on vocational training at skills centres, enabling youth and adults to gain employable abilities and achieve sustainable livelihoods.

Food Programs

Community food programs that support vulnerable families, reducing hunger, and strengthening health and wellbeing in communities.
