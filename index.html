Our Mission

Planting seeds of change
in every community

Founded on the belief that every person deserves dignity, security, and opportunity, Harbour of Hope works alongside communities to create lasting, meaningful change.

Food Security

Providing nutritious meals and groceries to families facing food insecurity, ensuring no one goes hungry in our community.

Housing Support

Helping individuals and families find safe, stable housing through emergency assistance, transitional programs, and advocacy.

Education

Empowering youth and adults with tutoring, mentorship, and scholarship programs that unlock pathways to brighter futures.

Community Care

Building networks of support through counselling, wellness programs, and social connection events for people of all ages.
